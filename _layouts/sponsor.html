Students can be a difficult demographic to advertise to, and that’s where CSS comes in. As a student-run society, we are uniquely poised to advertise your company’s internships and graduate schemes to our membership of nearly a thousand students from one of the world’s top 50 universities.

Why CSS?

All computer science students at the University of Bristol are members of CSS. This gives us over 600 undergraduates, 250 postgraduates, and a large network of alumni in our 2600+ member Facebook group.

Sponsorship is an effective way to promote your brand and attract top talent—we ensure you are visible to students throughout the year. Many of our students go on to intern and work at companies that sponsor us.
